# Chrome Web Store Listing Copy

## Name
QueueTube

## Short description (up to 132 chars)
Queue your YouTube subscriptions into playlists by tag, unwatched status, or all loaded videos.

## Detailed description
Tired of clicking each video one by one in your subscriptions? QueueTube adds queue controls to your YouTube subscriptions page so you can choose all loaded videos, only unwatched videos, or videos from tagged channels and then autoplay them in order with full skip and navigation controls.

Features:
▶️ Two-step queuing - choose Queue Subscriptions or Queue Unwatched, then choose #everything or one of your saved tags
🏷️ Channel tags - capture your subscriptions and organize channels into tags like #gaming, #music, or #news
👁️ Queue Unwatched - queue only videos with no visible YouTube watch-progress bar
⏭️ Autoplay - when a video ends, the next one starts automatically
⏮️ Skip controls - jump forward or back in your queue at any time
📋 Queue panel - shows all queued videos with titles so you know what's coming next
🔴 Watch progress bars - videos you've partially watched show a red progress bar in the queue panel, just like YouTube's own thumbnails, so you can decide whether to skip
🖱️ Click to jump - click any video in the panel to skip straight to it
📜 Load more with scrolling - scroll down your subscriptions before queueing to load even more videos (you can queue 300+ this way!)
🔀 Guilt-free sidebar browsing -- spot something interesting in the sidebar? Click it and watch it normally. Your queue is still saved, so just hit play in the panel to jump straight back to where you were. Or open it in a new tab to keep your queue playing uninterrupted in the original tab

New in v1.1.0:
🏷️ Channel tags - organize your subscribed channels into tags and queue only videos from the channels you want
👁️ Queue Unwatched - skip videos you have already started. QueueTube reads YouTube's own progress bars and filters them out automatically
🌐 #everything - use the reserved all-channels tag with either Queue Subscriptions or Queue Unwatched
🧾 Clear channel labels - see friendly channel names and handles together, such as P!NK [@pinkvideovault]
🔄 Continue Previous Queue - closed your tab halfway through? Return to your subscriptions page and pick up exactly where you left off
🎧 Media key and headphone support - use your keyboard media keys or headphone button to skip to the next video without switching to Chrome. Works in the background while you game, work, or browse elsewhere

Privacy:
QueueTube requests the storage permission and access to youtube.com. Storage is used only for QueueTube's local queue, captured channels, saved tags, and saved queue position. YouTube access is used to add QueueTube controls to YouTube pages and read the subscription/channel information needed for queueing. QueueTube does not access your Google account, does not collect analytics or telemetry, and does not transmit data to any server. QueueTube-owned data is stored locally in the extension's storage and never leaves your device.
